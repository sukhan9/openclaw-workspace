About Us

Preserving the Art of Indian Mithai

For generations, Shree Krishna Sweets has been the heart of Karimpur, West Bengal, serving the most authentic Indian sweets crafted with love and tradition. Our expert halwais (sweet makers) have mastered recipes passed down through centuries, ensuring every bite carries the essence of Indian culinary heritage.

We believe in purity above all. Every sweet is prepared using fresh, handpicked ingredients — from the finest khoya and pure desi ghee to premium dry fruits and fragrant cardamom. Our commitment to hygiene is unwavering, with our kitchens maintaining the highest standards of cleanliness. The result? That unmistakable homemade taste that transports you back to your grandmother's kitchen, where love was the secret ingredient in every preparation.
